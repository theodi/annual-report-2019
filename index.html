We spoke with lots of people about important data issues…

We conducted a YouGov poll, and found that nearly 9 in 10 people (87%) feel it is important or very important that organisations they interact with use data about them ethically.

Read more

Building on our work in data ethics, we’ve widened our conversations, and asked more people how they feel about data ethics and rights issues.

We also ran workshops with the public, with the RSA and Luminate, and.found that people want more agency over how data about them is shared.
